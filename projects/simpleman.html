Simpleman

By: Justin Oblak

Play!

Simpleman was an attempt to build a back-to-the-basics platformer. The controls and gameplay are simple: use the left and right arrows to move, 'X' to jump, 'Z' to attack, and space to use any items that you retrieve. The game follows a simple, 4 level, linear difficulty path while following an implicit storyline that the player learns upon completion.

I wanted to the form of Simpleman to follow the function. So, the art is simple: big, geometric shapes with bold, bright colors, and pops of detail to really bring everything together.

The end result is a fun, super playable platform that anyone can enjoy.

Features at a Glance:

  • 4 levels that scale in difficulty
  • 2 items
  • Component-based entity system
    • Allows for 'breathing' effect
    • Allows for equipping items dynamically
    • Could be expanded to allow for outfits, hats, etc
