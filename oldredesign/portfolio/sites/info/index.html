why most small to medium sized businesses should update their web presence

Customer Trends

The demographics that shape your user base are changing.
There are three key trends that every business owner needs to be on top of.

1.) Mobile

  • Mobile web traffic is exploding - up over 100% from 2011
  • Your site needs to be responsive - easily viewed both on a desktop/laptop and mobile devices
  • What does your website look like on an iPhone?
  • Non-responsiveness can lead to missed opportunities if customers get lost on your site using their iPad or iPhone

2.) Tech-saavy users

  • Customers no longer tolerate websites with outdated designs and clunky user interfaces
  • Web technology has evolved greatly; new techniques for customer attention and retention have been proven

3.) Social

  • These days everyone is a social media user.
  • Untapped potential for referrals, PR, and increasing lifetime value
